Lasagna Recipe

Home Page

Description:

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg

Instructions:

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
